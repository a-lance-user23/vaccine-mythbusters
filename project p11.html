Vaccine Mythbusters

Vaccinemythbusters is a website that will be dedicated to debunking false claims about the covid vaccine and encouraging people to take them. I will be sourcing the myths from friends and social media to find the most widespread and harmful misconceptions and deal with them. The last page of the website will be used to show the references for the information that was gathered and used in the debunking process. I also plan to share information on the virus and the danger that vaccine hesitancy could cause in the future.
